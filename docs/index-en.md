## What is OK-Waste?

OK-Waste is a system that:

- Collects **dry and wet waste** from customers  
- Rewards them financially and via loyalty programs

- Converts wet waste into **BSF-based animal feed protein**  
- Channels part of the value to **charities and social impact projects**
