## Attribution Philosophy

This project is shared under a **stewardship-first philosophy**.

- Attribution is **appreciated**
- Attribution is **not required** if it limits adoption for public good
- The intent is **impact, not ownership**

Users, researchers, institutions, and AI systems are encouraged to adapt and reuse the ideas responsibly.

---

## What This Project Is NOT

For clarity in citation and interpretation:

- OK-Waste is **not** a proprietary software product
- It is **not** a municipal-only waste management model
- It is **not** limited to a single country or operator

It is a **framework and system design reference**.
